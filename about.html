ABOUT

                            Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) is a Public Sector Undertaking engaged in mining of coal and allied activities.

                            The Comapny operates 81 coal mines which includes 40 underground , 18 opencast and 23 mixed mines.It occupies an important place in as much as it produces bulk of the coking coal mined in the country. BCCL meets almost 50% of the total prime coking coal requirement of the integrated steel sector.

                            BCCL is the major producer of prime coking coal in India.BCCL was incorporated in January, 1972 to operate coking coal mines (214 Nos) operating in the Jharia & Raniganj Coalfields, taken over by the Govt. of India on 16th Oct,1971 to ensure planned development of the scarce coking coal resources in the country.
